Kiss Me Killer

Introducing Kiss Me Killer, the electrifying embodiment of rebellion and raw energy hailing from the vibrant streets of Bristol. With influences ranging from the punk ethos of the Subhumans to the dark allure of psychobilly, and the kaleidoscopic sounds of 60s psychedelia, to the gritty spirit of LA punk rock, they are the sonic revolutionaries of their generation.

Formed in the crucible of a city steeped in musical history, Kiss Me Killer channels the ferocity of punk defiance into their explosive performances. Their music is a sonic cocktail of frenetic guitar riffs, thunderous basslines, and primal drumbeats that captivate audiences and ignite mosh pits wherever they go.

Nat, the enigmatic guitarist of Kiss Me Killer, brings a unique blend of punk ferocity and psychedelic flair to the stage. With lightning-fast riffs and hypnotic melodies, Nat's guitar work adds depth and dimension to the band's sound, captivating audiences with every chord.

Kat, the fiery frontwoman of Kiss Me Killer, commands the stage with her electrifying presence and powerhouse vocals. With a voice that cuts through the chaos like a razor blade, she delivers lyrics dripping with passion and rebellion, leaving audiences spellbound and craving more.

David Hammonds, the guitar virtuoso of Kiss Me Killer, channels the fury of punk and the soul of blues into every riff. With fingers that dance across the strings like lightning, he crafts sonic landscapes that captivate and electrify audiences, leaving them hungry for more of his raw, untamed talent.

A.J., the powerhouse drummer of Kiss Me Killer, drives the band's explosive energy with thunderous beats and relentless rhythms. With influences ranging from punk to psychobilly, A.J.'s dynamic style and infectious grooves keep the crowd pulsating, making every performance an unforgettable experience.
